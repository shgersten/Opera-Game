 This is an adventure game. Throughout the story you will be given
choices. Choose wisely for each choice has consequences. Beware 
of your prestige, wealth, health, music sheets and dresses. You 
need all In order to make it in Florence.....
The year is 1637. You are a young woman named Artemisia Fontana 
who studied for years under your father, a luthier name Giuseppe 
in Trento. You’ve always had a gift for singing and composing, a 
voice so grand your father knew it should be heard by the likes 
of Venetian nobility. Upon your eighteenth birthday your father 
has given you 5 scudi and reserved a place with a caravan heading 
to Venice. You’ve been told that your father once sold a violin to
the nobleman Filippo Bon of the Bon family to be given to one of 
his patronees. Filippo Bon lives in the Palazzo Priuli Bon near the 
newly created Teatro San Cassiano. He’s been in communication with
him and he says he is looking for the best artists, singers, and 
composers to expand his menagerie and will be holding auditions in 
two weeks. You must travel to his home on the Canal Grande in time 
for the performance. Your bags are packed with your compositions, 
a violin, some travel meals, and a small coin purse.
